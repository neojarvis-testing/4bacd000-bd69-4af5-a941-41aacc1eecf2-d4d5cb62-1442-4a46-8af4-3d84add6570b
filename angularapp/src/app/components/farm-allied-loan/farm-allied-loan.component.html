Farm Allied Activities Loan

Empowering agricultural ventures beyond traditional farming for a brighter future.

Farm allied activities loans are designed to support agricultural ventures beyond traditional farming, such as dairy, poultry, fisheries, and beekeeping. These loans typically cover expenses like infrastructure development, equipment purchase, and operational costs. Borrowers can benefit from flexible repayment options and competitive interest rates, with subsidies available under certain government schemes. Eligibility often requires proof of agricultural activity and basic documentation like income records and land ownership.

  • Support for ventures like dairy, poultry, fisheries, and beekeeping.
  • Coverage for infrastructure development, equipment, and operational costs.
  • Flexible repayment options with competitive interest rates.
  • Subsidies available under specific government schemes.
